What are the three doshas in Ayurveda?

expand_more

The three doshas—Vata (air and space), Pitta (fire and water), and Kapha (earth and water)—are the primary energies in our bodies. Understanding your dominant dosha can help you make lifestyle and dietary choices to maintain balance and wellness.
